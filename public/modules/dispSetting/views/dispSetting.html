Step 2 - Initial Map Positioning


You can set exactly where you would like the map to start and at what zoom level when customers visit your store locator page.


This is where you configure how your address and detailed information is presented on your store locator screen. The words in [square] brackets are tags and will merge in your actual data. The settings for "Address" will apply to the address listings on the left hand side of the Store Locator and the "Detailed" settings will apply to the information popup bubble on the map.

Once you set it how you like click Validate to ensure there are no errors and then click Preview to see what it will look like.

Here's a table of all the available tags: Information Tag Information Tag Store Name [name] Postal/Zip Code [postal_zip] Address [address] Phone [phone] Address 2 [address2] Fax [fax] City [city] Email [email] Province/State [prov_state] Hours of Operations [hours] Country [country] Website * [website] *These tags are only available in the Detailed template
